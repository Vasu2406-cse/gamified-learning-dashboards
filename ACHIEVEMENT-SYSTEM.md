# 🏆 Achievement System Guide

## 📊 How Achievements Work

Achievements are automatically awarded when you reach certain milestones!

---

## 🎯 Badge-Based Achievements

### 🥉 Badge Collector
- **Requirement**: Earn 2 badges
- **Description**: "Earned 2 badges! Keep collecting!"
- **How to get**: Complete quizzes and earn any 2 badges

### 🥈 Badge Hunter
- **Requirement**: Earn 4 badges
- **Description**: "Earned 4 badges! You're on fire!"
- **How to get**: Continue learning and earn 4 badges total

### 🥇 Badge Master
- **Requirement**: Earn 6 badges
- **Description**: "Earned 6 badges! You're a true master!"
- **How to get**: Earn 6 out of 8 available badges

### 👑 Ultimate Champion
- **Requirement**: Earn ALL 8 badges
- **Description**: "Earned ALL badges! You're the ultimate champion!"
- **How to get**: Unlock every single badge in the system

---

## 💎 Point-Based Achievements

### ⭐ Point Master
- **Requirement**: Earn 300+ points
- **Description**: "Earned 300+ points! You're a learning champion!"
- **How to get**: Answer 30 questions correctly (30 × 10 points)

### 🌟 Elite Learner
- **Requirement**: Earn 400+ points
- **Description**: "Earned 400+ points! You're among the elite!"
- **How to get**: Answer 40 questions correctly (40 × 10 points)

## 📈 Achievement Progression Path

### Beginner Path (0-100 points):
1. Complete first quiz → Get "First Steps" badge + achievement
2. Answer 10 correctly → Get "Accurate Answerer" badge + achievement
3. Earn 100 points → Get "Point Collector" badge + achievement
4. **Unlock**: Badge Collector achievement (2 badges earned)

### Intermediate Path (100-300 points):
5. Complete 5 quizzes → Get "Quiz Master" badge + achievement
6. Earn 250 points → Get "Point Hunter" badge + achievement
7. Answer 25 correctly → Get "Perfectionist" badge + achievement
8. **Unlock**: Badge Hunter achievement (4 badges earned)
9. **Unlock**: Point Master achievement (300+ points)

### Advanced Path (300-500 points):
10. Earn 500 points → Get "Champion" badge + achievement
11. Answer 50 correctly → Get "Legend" badge + achievement
12. **Unlock**: Badge Master achievement (6 badges earned)
13. **Unlock**: Elite Learner achievement (400+ points)
14. **Unlock**: Ultimate Champion achievement (all 8 badges)
